Banana Bread

A delicious and moist banana bread recipe that's perfect for breakfast or a snack.
To bake classic, moist banana bread, you will need simple ingredients like very ripe bananas, flour, butter, and eggs.
The process involves mixing the wet ingredients separately from the dry ingredients, combining them,
and then baking the batter in a loaf pan until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven and prepare pan: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    Grease an 8x4 inch or 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. Mash banana and mix with butter: Mash the bananas in a large bowl with a fork
    or potato masher until they reach your desired consistency.
  3. Mix in the remaining wet ingredients: Add the baking soda, salt, sugar,
    beaten egg, and vanilla extract to the banana mixture and stir until well combined.
  4. Add dry ingredients: Stir in the flour until the mixture is combined and no dry flour is visible.
    Be careful not to overmix the batter, as this can result in dense or dry bread.
  5. Pour the batter: into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula
  6. Bake for 55 to 65 minutes or until a wooden skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool the banana bread: In the pan on a wire rack for 10 minutes,
    then remove it from the pan to cool completely on the rack before slicing.
